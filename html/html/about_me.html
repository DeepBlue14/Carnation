Work

Robotics Laboratory

I am currently employed as an Undergraduate Researcher in Dr. Holly Yanco's Robotics Laboratory at the University of Massachusetts Lowell Computer Science Department. My job includes designing and implementing experimental software for robotic systems, running experiments, writing and publishing papers on my research, etc.

My work-related research includes user interfaces for assistive robotics, object segmentation, detection, and recognition, and mobile robotics. Many of these projects are developed using ROS, a meta-operating system for developing robotics applications. I have also explored the use of Android as a means of controlling robots.
